    New Mannat Home is a family-run homestay led by Raju Bhalla, offering a warm and welcoming ambience unlike any hotel. Experience the true spirit of Himachal Pradesh - it's not just a place to stay, it's a home away from home. Our convenient location in the heart of the valley puts you within easy reach of all the region's wonders. Explore the surrounding areas, or lace up your boots and embark on one of our many hiking trails. These scenic paths will lead you to historical treasures like ancient Naggar Castle, the captivating Roerich Art Gallery, and sacred old temples. Nestled amidst the majestic Pir Panjal range, New Mannat Home boasts breathtaking views of the Dholadhar range and the Beas River. Just a stone's throw away from the bustling towns of Manali and Kullu, our homestay offers the perfect balance of tranquility and accessibility. Meet your co-host, Urmil Bhalla, who delights in engaging conversations with guests. The warmth extends to the younger generation too, as Mannat and Raghav, Raju and Urmil's children, are also part of the New Mannat Home family. Come experience the authentic way of life! Witness the magic of our traditional "tandoor" natural heating system and savor delicious home-cooked meals prepared with love. At New Mannat Home, we invite you to immerse yourself in the rich culture and stunning beauty of the Kullu Valley.
